The Current Challenge

The Reality for Young Adults

Many young adults have to navigate environments while constantly vulnerable to critical health threats. Too often, they do not notice an allergen's presence until it enters their system, causing immediate, avoidable damage to their health.

How it's Managed Now

Currently, mitigation is entirely reactive. Individuals try their best to avoid known danger zones. When a slip-up happens and severe reactions manifest, they are forced to rely on painful, stressful emergency medical interventions like an "EpiPen".

The AllergAir System

1

The Wearable Sensor

An aesthetic, compact device that easily clips to your jeans or handbag. It stays with you effortlessly, ensuring you never leave it behind.

2

Instant Alerts

The core chemical sensor recognizes micro-elements of your specific allergens. Upon detection, both the device and your phone vibrate in sync to warn you instantly.

3

Intelligent App

Log in, input your profile, and let the app track environmental fluctuations. View a personalized heat map showing exactly where and when your allergen levels peaked.

Proactive, Not Reactive

AllergAir works because it transforms how you interface with your environment. By acting as an early warning system, it intercepts danger before it reaches you. This means living life confidently, minimizing health risks, and drastically reducing your reliance on heavy allergy medications.
